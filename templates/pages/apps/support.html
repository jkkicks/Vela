Support Button

Configure the support button that appears on the welcome message. This button provides quick access to help for new members.

How it Works

The support button appears on the welcome message alongside other onboarding buttons. When clicked, it shows your custom support message to the member privately (ephemeral message).

After changing these settings, you must update or replace the welcome message for the changes to take effect. Go to the Welcome System app to update the message.

How it Works

The Sync service monitors your Discord server and database, automatically detecting when they're out of sync.

When inconsistencies are found (like approval messages that timed out), it automatically updates Discord to match your database records.

  • Approved users: Message updated to show green approval status with approver information
  • Denied users: Message updated to show red denied status
  • Pending users: Messages left unchanged with active buttons

This ensures your Discord server always reflects the true state of your data, providing a reliable single source of truth.

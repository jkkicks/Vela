How Onboarding Works

1. New members click the "Complete Onboarding" button in the welcome message

2. A modal form appears with your configured fields

3. When submitted, their information is saved to the database

4. Their server nickname is updated using your template (if enabled)

5. They receive the configured role (if enabled)

6. Members who already completed onboarding will see a message saying they're already onboarded
